New Map Showcase

Timberline Trail

The Timberline Trail was the very first backpacking trip I went on in 2015. My mom & I spent 4 days trekking around the volcano. This map kickstarts a new series I am making of famous long distance trails I love, and those all around the world.

Colorado Ranch Map

This 3D watercolor painted map captures all the topography and charm of a 6-mile-long private ranch located in Western Colorado. Turning landscape to life and serving as a central artpiece at the ranch lodge.

North Bend & Snoqualmie Pass

This 3D-rendered terrain map captures the sweeping valleys and rugged peaks of the North Bend region with exquisite light and depth. Designed to evoke the feeling of flight over the landscape, it highlights every ridge and river.

Great Sand Dunes Campground

This illustrated campground map blends clarity and charm, guiding visitors through campsites, trails, and amenities with an inviting, adventure-ready aesthetic. Designed for both orientation and storytelling, it captures the character of the landscape while making navigation effortless for every guest.
