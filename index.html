    About Me

    I'm a freelance cartographer based in Jackson, Wyoming, former NPS Cartographic Technician at Grand Teton National Park, thru-hiker, and earth scientist. I create maps that are equal parts scientifically rigorous and visually stunning: photorealistic 3D terrain, hand-drawn watercolor, and GIS-driven analysis for the clients and landscapes that deserve something extraordinary.
